# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place for builders, designers, reviewers,
testers, and people experimenting with AI-assisted development.

Participants are expected to treat each other with respect, patience, and good
faith. Strong technical disagreement is welcome; personal attacks are not.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially across experience levels and tool preferences.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and grounded in project goals.
- Credit other people's ideas and contributions.
- Protect private information and do not publish secrets, tokens, or personal
  data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith argument.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Abuse of issue, pull request, discussion, or review channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you see a problem, contact the maintainer through the repository's GitHub
channels. For security-sensitive reports, use `SECURITY.md`.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, discussions, documentation, and related community channels.
